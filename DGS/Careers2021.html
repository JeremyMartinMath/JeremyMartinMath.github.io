Math Careers in Industry Day 2021

The KU Math Department hosted its fifth annual Careers in Industry Day was held on November 20, 2021. Panelists included:

  • Rachel Hart (American Century Investments, Shawnee, KS)
  • Bradley Isom (T-Mobile, Overland Park, KS)
  • Kevin Li (Federal Home Loan Bank, Topeka, KS)
  • Catherine (Katy) Micek (3M, St. Paul, MN)
  • Luis Serrano (Zapata Computing, Toronto, Canada)
  • Andrew Steyer (Sandia National Laboratories, Albuquerque, NM)
