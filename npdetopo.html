Numerical PDEs and Geometry/Topology Seminar
Fall 2014

Organizers

Mat Johnson, Jeremy Martin

Participants

Andrew Steyer, Bennet Goeckner, Bin Wang, Jila Niknejad, Kyle Claassen, Kevin Adams

Goal

Bring together faculty and students from pure and applied mathematics to study the survey article D. Arnold, R. Falk and R. Winther, "Finite element exterior calculus: from Hodge theory to numerical stability," Bull. Amer. Math. Soc. 47 (2014), 281-354.

Logistics

We meet on Mondays, 2-3 PM, in Snow 306.
