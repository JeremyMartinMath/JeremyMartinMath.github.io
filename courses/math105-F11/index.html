Sunday, December 18: Final grades are now available. The "Final Semester Score" on MyMathLab includes everything -- homework (15%), three tests (15% each), final exam (40%), the improvement bonus, and the extra credit assignment. Official grades will be available on KYou either tomorrow or Tuesday.

Additional information:

  • I decided to draw the grade lines lower than the standard 900/800/700/600, as follows:
    • A: 890 and up (24.4% of students)
    • B: 775-889 (38.6%)
    • C: 655-774 (27.0%)
    • D: 500-654 (6.6%)
    • F: 0-499 (3.4%)
    Due to the high number of extra credits we received, we did not grade the assignments submitted by students who were not close to a grade line (for instance, if your final score was 800/1000 without the extra credit). If you are one of those students and you would like me to look at your extra credit, let me know by e-mail. It may take some time for me to post graders' comments on extra-credit problems.

Friday, December 16: I have just posted the scores on the final exam on MyMathLab. I decided to discard seven of the lowest-scoring problems and gave everyone an additional 20-point bonus (out of 400). The mean was 78.1%.

The "Overall Score" is your total for the semester. If this is at least 900, 800, 700, or 600 points, you have earned at least an A, B, C or D respectively.

We are still grading extra-credit assignments, so grades are not posted yet. Our priority is to grade the assignments submitted by students who are close to a grade line.
