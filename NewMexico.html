    Schedule of Events

    Each talk is scheduled for 20 minutes, with 10 minutes for questions and a break.

    All talks will be in Room 226, Dane Smith Hall. The official session schedule on the AMS website, and appears below in compact form.

    Speakers are invited to e-mail Jeremy Martin links to their talk slides if/when available.

    • 10:30-10:50: Multilateral basic hypergeometric summation identities and hyperoctahedral group symmetries, Hasan Coskun (1059-05-179) [canceled]
